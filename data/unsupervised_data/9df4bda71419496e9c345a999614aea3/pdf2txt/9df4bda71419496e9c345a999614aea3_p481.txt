11. MOVEMENTS IN PROPERTY, PLANT AND EQUIPMENT

During the nine months ended September 30, 2018, the Group had additions of property, plant and equipment

of approximately RMB350,639,000 (2017: RMB130,845,000) in order to upgrade its manufacturing capabilities.

In addition,

the Group disposed of certain property, plant and equipment with a carrying amount of

approximately RMB74,000 through disposal of a subsidiary.
