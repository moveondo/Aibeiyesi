RESTRICTIONS ON THE CORNERSTONE INVESTORS

Each of the Cornerstone Investors has agreed that it will not at any time during the period
of six months starting from and inclusive of the Listing Date (the “Lock-up Period”), dispose
of any of the Offer Shares they have purchased pursuant to the relevant Cornerstone Investment
Agreements, save for certain limited circumstances, such as transfers to any of its affiliate
wholly-owned by the same parent entity of the Cornerstone Investor who will be bound by the
same obligations of such Cornerstone Investor, including the Lock-up Period restriction.
