For the year ended December 31, 2016, we recorded other gains of approximately
RMB15.1 million. For the year ended December 31, 2017 and the six months ended June 30,
2018, we recorded other losses of approximately RMB24.6 million and RMB4.8 million,
respectively.

Our interest income from debt investment was stable at approximately RMB0.3 million
in 2016 and 2017. Our net loss on disposal of debt investment increased from nil for the six
months ended June 30, 2017 to a loss of approximately RMB0.3 million for the six months
ended June 30, 2018, mainly due to the disposal of our investments in publicly traded corporate
bonds in March 2018.
