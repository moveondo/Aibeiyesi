management companies.

Ally Bridge LB Healthcare Master Fund Limited

Ally Bridge LB Healthcare Master Fund Limited (“Ally Bridge LB”), an exempted Company
incorporated in the Cayman Islands on March 19, 2015, is managed by Ally Bridge LB Management
Limited. Ally Bridge LB is a long-bias public equity fund focusing in Asia/Greater China healthcare.
The fund primarily invests in public equities, but also selectively participates in high-quality pre-IPO
deals. The fund invests across the entire healthcare value chain, in pharmaceuticals, biotech, medical
devices, distribution, hospitals and mobile health.
